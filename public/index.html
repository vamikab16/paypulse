      Core Innovation

      How PayPulse Detects Risk Before Default

      • When an SME borrower faces cash flow pressure, they begin selectively prioritising which suppliers to pay — a behaviour we call "payment triage."
      • Critical suppliers (materials, key services) get paid on time. Others are systematically delayed.
      • This creates a measurable divergence in payment timing across the supplier portfolio — a signal PayPulse detects automatically.
      Gap in Market

      The Blind Spot in Traditional Credit Monitoring

      • Traditional banking relies on: loan repayment status, revenue trends, and credit scores.
      • An SME can appear GREEN on all these metrics while quietly stretching supplier payments — a leading indicator of distress.
      • By the time loan payments are missed, the stress has been building for weeks or months. The intervention window has closed.
      NatWest Advantage

      Proactive Risk Detection for NatWest

      • PayPulse analyses supplier payment patterns week by week across the borrower's key relationships.
      • Detects when the spread between fastest-paid and slowest-paid suppliers starts diverging.
      • Flags stress 4-8 weeks before traditional credit metrics deteriorate.
      • Enables NatWest to intervene proactively — reducing defaults, protecting lending relationships, and supporting SME recovery.

      NatWest Risk Classification

      • LOW — All suppliers paid within normal terms. No triage behaviour. Standard monitoring.
      • MEDIUM — Payment triage detected. Some suppliers delayed. Proactive RM outreach recommended.
      • HIGH — Severe payment stress. Multiple suppliers significantly delayed. Escalate to Credit Committee.

      Business Value for NatWest

      • Reduce SME loan defaults by detecting stress weeks before traditional signals.
      • Lower provisioning costs through earlier, more effective intervention.
      • Strengthen SME relationships by offering proactive support before crisis.
      • Scalable across the portfolio — automated monitoring of thousands of SME accounts.
